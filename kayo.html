Biography
"KAY/O comes from an Alternate Timeline Earth where Radiants were enemies of humans and were being used as weapons. He may have arrived from his timeline using a specialized teleporter that took him back in time to Earth 1, arriving on board the destroyed Kingdom container ship "Meridian" at an island in the Atlantic Ocean."

KAY/O is a machine of war built for a single purpose: neutralizing radiants. His power to suppress enemy abilities cripples his opponents' capacity to fight back, securing him and his allies the ultimate edge.
